About Us

Our History

Founded in 2020, Anand Restaurant started as a small family-owned restaurant with a mission to provide high-quality, fresh meals in a cozy environment. What began as a dream has quickly turned into a beloved local establishment known for its innovative dishes and warm hospitality.

Over the years, we've grown from a single location to multiple branches across the city, all while maintaining the core values that have made us successful: dedication to quality, exceptional customer service, and a commitment to our community.

Our Mission

At Anand Restaurant, our mission is to create unforgettable dining experiences through our passion for food and dedication to excellence. We strive to use only the freshest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible, and to offer a menu that caters to diverse tastes and dietary needs.

We believe in the power of food to bring people together and aim to provide a welcoming space where guests can relax, enjoy great food, and create lasting memories with family and friends.
